FAQ

What keys are supported?

MetalForge supports E, Eb, D, C#, C, B, A, G, and F. All chord progressions and voicings automatically transpose to your selected root key.

The app isn't showing my saved favourites — what do I do?

Favourites are stored locally on your device. If they've disappeared, check that you're signed into the same Apple ID and haven't recently reinstalled the app. If the issue persists, contact support.
