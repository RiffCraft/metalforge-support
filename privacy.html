Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

MetalForge does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Overview

MetalForge ("the App") is developed and maintained by Florian Bacher ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

MetalForge is a music theory tool for guitarists. It works entirely on-device and does not connect to any external servers or services — with the exception of Apple's own App Store infrastructure for in-app purchases.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically, the App does not collect:

· Your name, email address, or any contact information
· Location data
· Usage analytics or crash reports
· Device identifiers
· Any data linked to your identity

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism:

· Your saved favourite chord progressions
· Your selected root key and style preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

In-App Purchases

MetalForge offers a one-time Pro upgrade via Apple's StoreKit framework. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or process any payment or financial information.

For details on how Apple handles purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13. The App is rated 4+ on the App Store.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
